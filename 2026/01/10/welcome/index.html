Greetings and Expectations

Welcome to my new blog/portfolio site! This will mark my journey into a deeper and more serious approach to my career as not just a penetration tester, but a security researcher. I will continue releasing writeups for different CTFs and platforms, but on top of that I will also touch on topics that I find interesting. As I read through different books on topics like C Programming, Operating Systems, Binary Exploitation, or even Reverse Engineering, I will try and bring my perspective and takeaways from said books. I will attempt to combine my over one year of experience as both a self-taught Penterster and a Cybersecurity student to provide beginners and intermediates with plenty of information and guidance. I would prefer if the people who read my blog utilize it as a learning tool and not just a simple copy and paste for CTFs. I encourage everyone to do their own research on any topics where you feel my posts lack clarity or detail.

Legacy Site

For those of you who are coming from my legacy site, I would also like to welcome you and thank you for continuing to follow me. You guys already know what to expect and I hope you’re happy with the new site. For the people who aren’t familiar, my first blog was on Wordpress. I started it on December 2024 and it was a great way to dip my feet into the world of writeups and blogs. I wrote an old guide to getting started in Cybersecurity and another for my thoughts on TryHackMe. As you can imagine my opinions on both of those topics have changed since. If you’re interested in checking my old site, you can do so by clicking the button below.
